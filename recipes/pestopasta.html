Pesto Pasta Recipe

The world's easiest pasta

A simple pasta that will take you only fifteen minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 (16 ounce) box dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1/4 cup butter, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup pesto

Directions

  1. Step 1 - Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a rolling boil.
  2. Step 1 - Cook the fettuccini at a boil until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain. Transfer the fettuccini to a bowl.
  3. Step 2 - Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Step 3 - Cook the garlic and pesto in the melted butter until warmed, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Step 4 - Pour over the fettuccini and toss to coat.
