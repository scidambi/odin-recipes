Cheese Omelette Recipe

The world's yummiest omelette

A timeless recipe that will take you under half an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 portobello mushroom cap, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded low-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon prepared pesto

Directions

  1. Step 1 - Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Step 2 - Cook the portobello mushroom and red onion in the olive oil until the mushroom has softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3 - Whisk the egg whites and water together in a small bowl; pour over the mushroom and onion mixture.
  4. Step 4 - Season the egg whites with salt and pepper.
  5. Step 5 - Cook, stirring occasionally, until the egg whites are no longer runny, about 5 minutes.
  6. Step 6 - Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the mixture; top with the pesto.
  7. Step 7 - Fold the omelette in half and continue cooking until the cheese melts, 2 to 3 minutes.
